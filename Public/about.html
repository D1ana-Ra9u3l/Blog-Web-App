Why Computer Programming?

If my passion is in engineering -you would say- why doing computer programming? Well, computer science is an ever increasing need in today's world and I am a firm believer of making tasks more efficiently by getting computers to work for you. I am knowledgeable in the field of organizing a production line, and I know that one can 10x the production of any activity by simply getting the right tools that fit the needs of the activity. Which is why I started learning computer programming: I wanted to be able to make the computer do exactly what I wanted, so that I could complete my tasks more efficiently. And that is what I can do for your company too, I can create the exact software YOU need in order to optimize and expand your activity!
